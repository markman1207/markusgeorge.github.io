History of myself

A few things to note

  1. Date of birth: Dec 7, 2006
  2. I have only lived in California since 2013.
  3. My family is from Maryland and Oakland.

I was born in Hampton,Virginia on Dec 7,2006. My parents were still in college, so my grandma watched me during the day. I don't remember a lot happening at that time, so I'm just going to skip ahead to when I was about 3 when we moved to Maryland. While I was in Maryland, the biggest thing that I remember happening was going to Washington D.C to watch the Washington Nationals play. I remember the stadium looking like a giant spaceship because I was so young and so small. I lived with my mom,dad,aunt,grandpa and grandma along with my brother. When I was about 6, we moved here to Los Angeles.

When I moved to Los Angeles, I skipped a grade so instead of going to Kindergarden, I went straight to first grade at Windsor Hills Elementary school. I went there for a year before going to El Rincon Elementary School from 2nd to 5th Grade. When I graduated Elementary School I went to Culver City Middle School.
